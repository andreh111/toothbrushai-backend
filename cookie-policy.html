1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ToothBrush AI ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our mobile application and related services. This policy explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

2. What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website or use an app. Similar technologies include:

  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device
  • Session Storage: Temporary data stored during your app session
  • Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers for your device
  • Analytics IDs: Identifiers used for app analytics
  • Push Notification Tokens: Tokens for sending notifications
