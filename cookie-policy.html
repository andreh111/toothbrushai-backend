1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains ToothBrush AI's approach to cookies and similar technologies. Since our mobile application operates entirely offline and doesn't collect or transmit any user data, we have minimal use of these technologies. This policy explains our privacy-focused approach.

2. What Technologies We Use

Minimal Technology Usage: ToothBrush AI uses only essential local storage technologies and does not use cookies, tracking, or analytics.

The only technologies we use are:

  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device for app functionality
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences stored locally

We do NOT use:

  • Cookies: No web cookies or tracking cookies
  • Device Identifiers: No unique device tracking
  • Analytics IDs: No analytics or usage tracking
  • Push Notification Tokens: No push notifications or tokens
  • Session Tracking: No session or usage monitoring
